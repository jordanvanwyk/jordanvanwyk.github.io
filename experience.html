Sumo Bot - Solid Edge, Pic Basic Pro, Traxmaker
The sumo bot is a robot designed to push other bots out of a sumo-style ring. The chassis design was created in Solid Edge, circuit boards designed with Traxmaker, and programmed in Pic Basic Pro. IR sensors are used to detect other bots, and phototransistors are used to detect the ring's white edge. A custom H-Bridge is used to step-up the motor's voltage to 12v for increased speed.
