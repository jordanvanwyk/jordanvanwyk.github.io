Projects

Firefighter - Solid Edge, Pic Basic Pro, Traxmaker
A robot that navigates a maze to find and extinguish a candle. The chassis design is created in Solid Edge, circuit boards made with Traxmaker, and is coded with Pic Basic Pro.
Sumo Bot - Solid Edge, Pic Basic Pro, Traxmaker
The sumo bot is a robot designed to push other bots out of a sumo-style ring. The chassis design is created in Solid Edge, circuit boards made with Traxmaker, and is coded with Pic Basic Pro. IR sensors are used to detect other bots, and a phototransistor are used to detect the ring's white edge. An H-Bridge is used to up the motor's voltage to 12v.
Voice Module - AutoCad Fusion 360, ARDUINO, MasterCam
The Voice Module was created under the supervision of Dr. Sean Peterson. Developed an interactive 3D model of a human vocal tract to give insight into how humans create vowels and recognisable sounds from the vibrations of vocal folds.The module was designed in AutoCad Fusioin 360 and then parts were 3D printed or CNC'd and assembled. The sound source is an ARDUINO connected to an amplified speaker.
LED Cube - Pic Basic Pro, Traxmaker
The LED cube is a 5x5x5 array of LED's controlled by a Pic16f887 microcontroller. Persistance of vision (POV) is used to light up individual rows in order to prevent interference, and PWM is used to control brightness.
